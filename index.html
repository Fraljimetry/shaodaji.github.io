Expository note · July 2026

The Gaussian Curvature Formula for Graphs of Functions on R²

The note computes the curvature of the graph of a twice continuously differentiable function by differentiating the global unit normal and representing the resulting linear map in a tangent basis. At a critical point, the formula reduces to the determinant of the Hessian.
