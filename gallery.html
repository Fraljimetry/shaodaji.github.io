Visualization

Mandelbrot Set

A mathematical visualization generated with my graphing calculator.

Mandelbrot Set

This rendering comes from the iteration zn+1 = zn2 + c. A simple recurrence produces a boundary with intricate, self-similar structure at every scale.

The image was produced in the complex-number mode of my graphing calculator.

Calligraphy

A Moonlit Night on the Spring River

A handwritten transcription of the celebrated poem by Zhang Ruoxu.

A Moonlit Night on the Spring River · Zhang Ruoxu

This long horizontal work transcribes the Tang-dynasty poem in vertical columns. The photograph has been cropped tightly around the calligraphy sheet so the writing, spacing, and movement of the brush remain the focus.
